About the African Market Data Platform

Our Mission

The African Market Data Platform is dedicated to democratizing access to high-quality, reliable financial and macroeconomic data from African markets. We aim to empower investors, analysts, fintechs, researchers, and other financial stakeholders with the accurate information they need to make informed decisions.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Market Data

Access financial data from major African exchanges including JSE (South Africa), NGX (Nigeria), and BRVM (West Africa). Get historical price data, indices, company information, and more.

Daily Market Summaries

Stay informed with our comprehensive daily market summaries that analyze market movements, highlight top performers, and provide insights into market sentiment across African exchanges.

API Access

Integrate African market data into your own applications with our comprehensive API. Access endpoints for exchanges, tickers, prices, indices, and macroeconomic indicators.

Customizable Alerts

Set up personalized alerts based on price movements, earnings releases, or macroeconomic data to stay on top of market changes and opportunities.

Our Data Sources

We collect data from multiple authoritative sources to ensure comprehensiveness and accuracy:

  • Stock Exchange Websites - Direct data from JSE, NGX, BRVM, and other African exchanges
  • Central Bank Data - Monetary policy information, interest rates, and forex data
  • Company Investor Relations - Earnings reports, corporate actions, and announcements
  • African News Sources - Market news and sentiment analysis
  • Statistical Agencies - Macroeconomic indicators and demographic data

All data is collected and processed through our ETL pipeline to ensure quality, consistency, and timeliness before being made available on our platform.
